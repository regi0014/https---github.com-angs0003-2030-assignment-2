Passing the VORT Exam

Welcome to the VORT Exam Preparation Website

Prepare for your VORT exam with these essential tips and tricks:


  • 1. Log Book : Only attempt the VORT exam if you have completed all your required driving hours.
  • 2. Driving Tips and Techniques: You should be familiar with your hand placement while driving (10 and 2).
  • 3. Parking Tutorials: Practice parking maneuvers to ace the exam.
  • 4. Maneuvers: You should be familiar with the maneuvers you are expected to do in the VORT exam.
  • 5. Road Rules: You should have a thorough understanding of the road rules.

  • If you have completed all your driving hours and are confident with your driving skills, please book your VORT exam
